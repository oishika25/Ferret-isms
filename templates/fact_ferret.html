Fun Facts About Ferrets

"Knowledge is Power"

  • Ferrets Can catch Influenza from humans
  • Ferrets are obligated carnivores and cannot survive without meat in their diet
  • Ferrets have two vertebrae in their spine, one in their neck and the other in their tail
  • Ferrets have a four hour gut transit time
  • Ferrets are crepuscular, they are awake most during dusk and dawn and sleep during other hours
  • Ferrets are a part of the Mustelidae family (like Polecat) but are often mistaken for rodents
  • Ferrets are naturally inclined to defecate in corners
  • Ferrets are susceptible to hairballs. Strongly recommend Lax treats
  • Wysong digestive support 90 is the best dry ferret food in the market today
  • When ferrets get happy and excited they start to jump around. This is called a warrior dance
  • When ferrets move from one home to another, it decreases their lifespan due to the new environment stress
  • Ferrets are related to pole cats
